WartaSeni Privacy Policy

Welcome to WartaSeni! This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our platform ("Platform").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our Platform:

  • Personal information you provide during registration, such as your username and email address.
  • Information about your activity on the Platform, such as the artwork you upload and the content you view.
  • Device information, such as your IP address and browser type.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Platform.
  • To personalize your experience on the Platform.
  • To send you important information about the Platform, such as updates and notices.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Platform.

We will not share your information with any third-party for marketing purposes without your consent.

4. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • You can access and update your information at any time.
  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us.
  • You can request that we delete your information.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is ever 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the revised Policy on the Platform. You are expected to check this page periodically so you are aware of any changes, as they are binding on you.
