Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How to upload artwork?

To upload artwork, go to the Upload page and follow the instructions provided.

How to edit my profile?

You can edit your profile by clicking the "Edit Profile" button.

How to leave a comment on an artwork?

To leave a comment on an artwork, go to the artwork's page and use the comment section below the artwork.

How to share an artwork?

You can share an artwork by clicking the share icon on the artwork's page and selecting the platform you want to share it on.

How to change my password?

To change your password, go to your profile and click the "Edit Profile" button.

How to delete my account?

To delete your account, go to the account settings and select the "Delete Account" option. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the deletion.

How to contact support?

If you need further assistance, you can contact our support team via the "Contact Us" page. *For registered users only.
