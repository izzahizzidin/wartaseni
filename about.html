About WartaSeni

WartaSeni is a web-based platform dedicated to connecting Malaysian artists and art enthusiasts. We provide a safe and supportive space for artists to showcase their digital artworks, connect with a wider audience, and receive valuable feedback. Art lovers can discover new and established Malaysian artists, explore various styles, and support their favourites.

Our Mission:

  1. Empowering Malaysian Digital Artists: To empower Malaysian artists by providing them with a platform to share their work, gain recognition, and connect with a global audience.
  2. Fostering a Thriving Digital Art Community: To foster a thriving digital art community in Malaysia by encouraging collaboration, communication, and support among artists and art lovers.
  3. Promoting Malaysian Digital Art on the World Stage: To promote Malaysian digital art on a global scale, showcasing the talent and creativity of Malaysian artists to a wider audience.

Our Passion:

We are passionate about art and believe in its power to connect people, spark creativity, and inspire. We are committed to providing the best possible experience for Malaysian artists and art lovers alike, constantly working to improve our platform and expand its features.
